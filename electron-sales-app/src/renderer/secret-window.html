Secret Data Insertion Tool

This tool allows you to insert hundreds of sample data entries into your current workbook for testing purposes.

How It Works

  • This tool will insert realistic sample data into your currently loaded workbook
  • Data includes products, customers, sales, and invoices
  • Accounting data will be excluded as requested
  • All data is generated programmatically for testing purposes
  • Existing data in your workbook will not be affected
